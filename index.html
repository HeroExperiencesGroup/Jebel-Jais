The Opportunity

Today, Jebel Jais is home to some of the region's most successful adventure attractions. The opportunity ahead is far greater.

The next chapter is not about adding another attraction. It is about transforming Jebel Jais into a complete mountain destination — and using it as a catalyst for tourism growth across the emirate.

Desert & Heritage Experiences

The Al Wadi Desert is one of Ras Al Khaimah's greatest opportunities to create a world-class desert destination rooted in nature, culture, and conservation.

RAK is already advancing key initiatives, including a desert welcome centre and mountain biking trails. Hero's flagship opportunity is a Desert Sanctuary Camp — a premium, low-impact experience celebrating the heritage, landscapes, wildlife, and traditions of the Arabian desert.
